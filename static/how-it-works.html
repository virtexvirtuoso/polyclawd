Step 3: Size with Kelly

We use the Kelly Criterion ‚Äî a formula that answers: "Given my win rate and the payout, what % of my bankroll should I bet?"

We use 1/6 Kelly ‚Äî bet 1/6th of the mathematically optimal amount. Safe enough to survive bad streaks, aggressive enough to grow.
